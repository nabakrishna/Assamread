About

Do you remember when we used to get caught by parents while hiding a novel between the pages of a textbook, reading eagerly instead of following the lesson? Or the thrill of waiting for a book a friend was reading, or to read 'Rangmon' after my parents brought it home? Now, where has that passion for reading gone? Has it been lost to the demands of time, or the pull of our phones?

If you share the vision of revitalizing the reading culture, especially among the younger generation, join us in this effort. Let's gather, sit together, and read in silence, reigniting a shared love for books. Will you join us?
